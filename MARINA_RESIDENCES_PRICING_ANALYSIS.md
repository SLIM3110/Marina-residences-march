### Key Observations

1. **Listing vs Reality Gap:** Active listings are consistently priced **9-30% above** actual transacted prices. Buyers should target discounts of at least 5-10% below asking.

2. **Heavy Supply in 2-Bed Segment:** 182 active listings vs only 29 transactions in the data period indicates a buyer's market for 2-beds. Sellers need to price competitively.

3. **3-Bed B-Type is Undervalued in Listings:** Many B-type 3-beds are listed at AED 5.3M-6.5M but transactions show the AED 6.7M-7.3M range is achievable for good units. There may be buying opportunities here.

4. **A-Type 3-Bed is Tightly Held:** Fewer listings relative to demand, transactions at AED 10M-15.5M show strong pricing. Premium renovations justify AED 14M+.

5. **Penthouse Market is Illiquid:** Very few transactions, wide price ranges. Patience required from both buyers and sellers.

6. **View Premium Hierarchy:**
   - Full Sea View: Highest premium (~15-25% over community view)
   - Marina View: Strong premium (~10-15%)
   - Community/Pool/Partial: Baseline
   - No view / low floor: Discount of 5-15%

7. **Floor Level Impact:** Higher floors within the same building command a measurable premium. Floor 6+ with sea views achieve the best PSF rates. Floor 1-3 with community views represent entry-level pricing.

8. **Building Premium:** MR2 and MR4 tend to command slightly higher prices due to view orientations. MR3 has a concentration of listings at the lower price bands.
