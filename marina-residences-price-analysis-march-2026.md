## Key Takeaways

1. **2-Bedroom** is the most liquid category (75 sales, 774 rental records) - easiest to price confidently
2. **Furnished premium** is ~30% for 2BR (AED 283K vs 218K) and ~65% for 3BR (AED 690K vs 417K)
3. **New contracts command ~15% more** than renewals across all types
4. **3-Bedroom offers the best gross yield** at 6.0%, making it the strongest investment play
5. **2026 YTD shows softening** in 2BR sales (median AED 3.3M vs full-year AED 3.95M) but strong 3BR activity
6. **Most common 2BR rent** is firmly at AED 220K-230K/yr - this is the market sweet spot
7. **3BR rental market is bimodal** - standard units cluster around AED 280K-330K while premium/large units sit at AED 600K+
